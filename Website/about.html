Why Yalla.House Exists

The property market was broken.
So we fixed it.

Traditional estate agents charge 1–3% commission on your sale price. On a £400,000 home, that’s £6,000+ — for services that haven’t meaningfully changed in decades.

Meanwhile, sellers were chasing their agents for updates. Buyers were booking viewings for homes already reserved. Offers were logged on sticky notes. It was chaos — and everyone was paying for it.

Yalla.House changes all of this. We built the tools agents should have built — and made them available to everyone, at a fraction of the cost.

67% of buyers say slow or no reply is their #1 frustration

58% have viewed homes listed as "available" that were already reserved

What We Do Differently

Built on clarity, not commission

Every feature we build answers the same question: does this make the seller or buyer's life measurably better?

Flat Fees, Always

No percentage. No tiered commissions. You pay once, and keep every euro of your sale price — no matter what it sells for.

Weekly Progress, Automatic

Every seller receives a co-branded weekly update covering enquiries, viewings, feedback, and next steps. No chasing required.

Live Viewing Slots

You set when you're available. Buyers self-book confirmed slots — no email ping-pong, no double-booking, no no-shows.

Transparent Offer Management

Every offer is logged with timestamp, buyer status, proof of funds, and chain details. You see everything in real time.

Real-Time Portal Sync

Status changes update across Rightmove, Zoopla, and OnTheMarket automatically — no ghost listings, no stale data.

Secure & Compliant

All data handled in line with EU GDPR. Regulated payment partners. KYC/KYB verification for verified buyers and sellers.

Our Standards

The Yalla Code of Clarity

Every listing on Yalla.House operates under these commitments — measurable, automatic, no exceptions.

1

First Reply SLA

All enquiries acknowledged within 2 business hours. Measured automatically in the platform.

2

Accurate Status

Reserved / SSTC status updated within 4 hours of any change — synced to all portals instantly.

3

Viewing Clarity

Live viewing slots or three specific time options within 24 hours of any request. No more email ping-pong.

4

Offer Transparency

Every offer logged with financing evidence and an acknowledgement timestamp. Owners see a verified offer board.

5

Weekly Vendor Update

Auto-sent every 7 days with enquiries, viewings, feedback, and next steps. Zero manual effort required.
