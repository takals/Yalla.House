    # **🔎 Yalla “Clarity Index” – Simulated Findings (n=400, UK, Jan 2026)**
    
    # **Home Hunters (n=250)**
    
    - 67% say slow or no reply is their #1 frustration.
    - 58% found homes listed as “available” that were actually under offer.
    - 55% felt booking a viewing was harder than it should be (back-and-forth emails).
    - 49% saw poor photos or missing floorplans.
    - 46% found offer steps unclear (who to send, what to include, proof required).
    - 38% said agent follow-ups felt pushy or irrelevant.
    - 33% said the negotiator didn’t know key details (tenure, service charge, EPC nuances).
    
    # **Owners / Sellers (n=150)**
    
    - 69% want a weekly progress snapshot as standard.
    - 57% felt “in the dark” about what was happening week-to-week.
    - 55% said they chased their agent for updates.
    - 48% said managing viewing times created avoidable admin.
    - 44% couldn’t see where offers stood (who’s ahead; what’s verified).
    - 41% had to re-enter property info in multiple places.

    # **🏅 “Code of Clarity” (Agent pledge you can roll out with Yalla)**
    
    Agencies on Yalla can adopt this lightweight pledge; it becomes a badge you show to sellers.
    
    1. SLA: First reply within 2 business hours (measured in Yalla).
    2. Accurate status: Update to Under Offer/SSTC within 4 hours of change.
    3. Viewing clarity: Share live slots or respond with three specific times within 24 hours.
    4. Offer transparency: Every offer logged with AIP/chain evidence and an acknowledgement timestamp.
    5. Weekly vendor update: Auto-sent every 7 days with enquiries, viewings, feedback, next steps.
    
    Yalla makes this easy (automations + dashboards); agents keep ownership (calls/WhatsApp/email go to them).

            - **Agent-managed:** agent proposes 2–3 slots → Owner one-tap approve/decline.

            - Auto email (co-branded): Enquiries, Viewings, Feedback bullets, Next steps, **“View live dashboard”** button.

        - **Offers:** captured via Offer Form link (or agent logs manually).
        - **Metrics we can show safely:** Owner dashboard opens, weekly email opens, enquiry count, viewing request→confirmed rate, clicks on Call/WhatsApp from Yalla emails.
            
            *(No unverified portal page-view numbers.)*

        - **Magic-link** with revocation + optional 6-digit passcode.
        - **Agent-controlled visibility** per listing (amounts & PII OFF by default).
        - **Direct Mode** by default: Call/WhatsApp/Email buttons go **directly to the agent**.

        - Owner dashboard loads < **1.5s** TTFB; works on mobile first.

        - Email lead parsing recognises Rightmove/Zoopla/OTM ADF and increments **Enquiries** within **60s**.

    # **🟥CATEGORY 1 — TECHNICAL RISKS**

    - DNS misconfiguration
    - Database downtime
    - Cloudflare outage
    
    **Impact:**
    
    ❗ Bookings stop
    
    ❗ Money lost
    
    ❗ Seller trust broken

    - Lock DNS editing to Founder only

    - Weekly health check of logs + Sentry

    ## **1.2 Booking System Failure**
    
    **Symptoms:** Viewings not saving, double bookings, wrong times.
    
    **Prevention:**
    
    - Automated integration test for booking flow
    - Sync check every 24h (CRON)
    - Keep availability logic simple (MVP)
    - Avoid conflicting calendars at first
    
    ---
    
    ## **1.3 Messaging Failure**
    
    **Symptoms:** WhatsApp not sending / email missing.
    
    **Prevention:**
    
    - Use **verified WhatsApp templates**

    - Build fallback email notification

    **Symptoms:** Packages cannot be purchased.
    
    **Prevention:**
    
    - Check webhook weekly
    - Enable Stripe “alerts”
    - Test £1 checkout monthly

    ## **1.5 Slow Pages / Broken Media**
    
    **Causes:**
    
    - Heavy images
    - Broken map provider
    - Low hosting tiers

    - Use automatic image compression
    - Limit uploads to <10 MB

## **🔌 2. How to Get Onto Rightmove, OnTheMarket, etc.**

You need a **gateway or licensed agent partner** — Rightmove does not accept listings directly from private individuals or unlicensed portals.

Three practical routes:

### **Option A – Partner with a Listing Aggregator (Fastest)**

- **FeedSync**, **PropertyHive**, or **99Home Partner API** can push your listings to all major portals.
- You pay per listing or per month (typically £10–£15 each).
- They appear on Rightmove with “listed by NoAgent / Owner-Managed.”
- Buyer inquiries are forwarded to your dashboard email endpoint.

→ **Result:** sellers see their listing live on national portals within 48 h.

### **Option B – Partner with a Licensed Agency (White-Label)**

- Find a small, tech-friendly estate agent willing to act as “distribution partner.”
