# One Important Rule

For SaaS:

**Color ratio**

```
70% neutral (white/grey)
20% dark (text/navigation)
10% brand color (orange)
```

If orange dominates, it looks **cheap or like a sale banner**.

---

# Extra Trick for Property Platforms

Use **colored numbers**.

Example:

```
Enquiries: 12  (orange)
Viewings: 6    (blue)
Offers: 2      (green)
```

That gives the dashboard life.
