# Pricing & Revenue Strategy
**Last updated:** 2026-04-15

## Owner Pricing (3 tiers)
- **Starter (£0 forever)**: free channels (OpenRent, Gumtree, FB), dashboard, agent proposals, inbox, AI desc, contractor marketplace
- **Sell Smart (£19/mo during sale)**: viewing calendar, 3D tour, weekly market price, buyer follow-ups, offer comparison, documents. Cancel when sale completes. Avg revenue: £19 × 4 months = £76
- **Home Passport (£5/mo forever)**: bookable services (cleaners, gardeners, handymen), away mode (home check-ins, pet/plant care), home value tracker, maintenance scheduler, documents, verified providers, EPC/insurance reminders

## Agent Pricing (3 tiers)
- **Starter (£0 forever)**: profile, receive briefs, reply to 5/mo, calendar, inbox
- **Pro (£49/mo)**: unlimited replies, CRM sync, priority matching, custom branding, doc sharing
- **Agency (£149/mo)**: 10 seats, team calendar, analytics, white-label portal, account manager

## Hunter Pricing (2 tiers)
- **Free**: passport, agent matching, browse listings, inbox, 3 saved searches, readiness badges
- **Hunter Pro (£9/mo)**: instant alerts, unlimited searches, area price reports, priority matching, viewing scheduler

## Revenue Lines Beyond Subscriptions
- **Mortgage broker referrals**: £200-500 per completed mortgage. Pursue from day one. No cost to hunter.
- **Solicitor/conveyancer**: free directory for owners (SRA-regulated). Revenue from solicitor side, not owner.
- **EPC bookings**: £15-20 referral per booking. Automated matching by postcode.
- **Photography/floor plans**: one-off bookings through dashboard. Commission from photographer.
- **Contractor marketplace**: free browse for all. Commission from service provider side (5-10%).
- **Agent connection fees (Phase 2+)**: £5-15 per introduction that converts. Zero during MVP/Phase 1.

## Key Decisions
- Rightmove/Zoopla: ONLY agents can list there. We never claim owners can.
- Agent contracts: we don't mention switching/removing agents. No conflict with agent's own contracts.
- "Verified" vs "registered": use "registered" for scraped agents until actual verification exists.
- Listing scraping: free/open sources (OpenRent, Gumtree, FB) are defensible. Daily frequency realistic.
- Country expansion: UK first (17K agents). Pipeline: DE (IVD), FR (FNAIM), NL (NVM), ES, IT.

## Readiness Badges (Hunter)
- Mortgage in Principle (referral revenue from broker)
- Identity Verified
- Profile Complete
- Renter Ready (references & deposit)

## Future Features (Parked)
- Shared home search / living community (beta)
- Special search criteria: wheelchair accessible, soundproof, EV charging, pet-friendly
- Airbnb-style short let management (high effort, future phase)
